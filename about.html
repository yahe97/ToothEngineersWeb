About Tooth Engineers

Born with the idea to generate content for showcasing our experiments and new innovations at the intersection of dentistry and bioengineering.

Our Story

It All Started With a Dream to Change the World

In the early 2010s, an aspiring dental student read about the transformative capabilities of 3D printing in the field of bone regeneration. At that moment, he decided he would not just become a neighborhood dentist. Instead, he found a more significant purpose in life: to become an innovator capable of revolutionizing the dental industry.

Dreaming big is one thing. The "doing" is what actually matters. To his credit, this dental student went on to complete dental school, a residency in prosthodontics, and a PhD in bioengineering. To do the math, that is an additional 12 years of academic life after college. Talk about dedication!

During his academic career, he met some like-minded dentists and bioengineers. The team that has been assembled is Tooth Engineers.

Tooth Engineers is a dental science-focused content creator. We are working to engineer smiles by combining the disciplines of dentistry and bioengineering. We will be providing content ranging from general oral health to more scientifically driven investigations of the world through experimentation.

We hope you all will follow us in our journey to innovate, educate, and create brighter smiles for a better tomorrow!
