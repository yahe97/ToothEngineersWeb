About Tooth Engineers

We exist at the intersection of dentistry and bioengineering — driven by a singular mission to engineer better smiles through science.

🦷
Our Story

Born at the Crossroads of Science and Dentistry

Tooth Engineers was founded by a dental professional who, inspired in the early 2010s by the transformative potential of 3D printing in bone regeneration, chose a different path than general practice. Rather than opening a typical dental clinic, our founder pursued dental school, a prosthodontics residency, and a bioengineering PhD — approximately 12 years of advanced education beyond undergraduate work.

That journey led to the creation of Tooth Engineers: a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between cutting-edge bioengineering research and everyday dental care. Our founder assembled a team of like-minded dentists and bioengineers who share the same conviction — that dentistry is on the cusp of a scientific revolution, and that the world deserves to know about it.

Today, Tooth Engineers produces content spanning general oral health education to scientifically rigorous experimental investigations, all united by one overarching goal: brighter smiles for a better tomorrow.

What We Stand For

Engineering Smiles by Combining Dentistry and Bioengineering

We are working to engineer smiles by combining the disciplines of dentistry and bioengineering. Our content spans accessible oral health guidance for the general public, all the way to scientifically rigorous experimental investigations for clinicians and researchers. We believe that when science is communicated clearly and compellingly, it creates lasting positive change.
